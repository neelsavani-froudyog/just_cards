# About Us

Welcome to **JustScans**.

JustScans is built to simplify the way professionals and teams capture, organize, and collaborate on business contacts.

In many business environments, people collect contacts through business cards, exhibitions, trade shows, meetings, conferences, and field visits. Managing those contacts later across individuals and teams often becomes messy, fragmented, and difficult to track. Valuable connections can get lost, forgotten, or remain siloed with one person.

JustScans is designed to solve that problem.

---

## What We Do

JustScans helps users:
- scan business cards quickly,
- extract contact information digitally,
- save and organize contacts in one place,
- create events for conferences, meetings, expos, or campaigns,
- create organizations for team-based collaboration,
- invite members by email,
- share relevant contacts within an event or organization,
- add notes, tags, and documents such as PDFs to contacts,
- maintain a structured and searchable professional contact database.

---

## Why We Built JustScans

Business networking is valuable only when contacts can be captured properly, remembered, followed up on, and shared with the right people.

Traditional methods often lead to:
- lost business cards,
- manual entry errors,
- scattered contact records,
- no team visibility,
- poor follow-up tracking,
- no event-wise organization,
- no central ownership of professional connections.

JustScans addresses these problems by turning physical business cards into a digital, collaborative, and usable contact system.

## Key Features

### 1. Business Card Scanning
Scan physical business cards and digitize contact details in a faster and more organized way.

### 2. Contact Management
Store and manage contact details, notes, tags, and supporting files in one place.

### 3. Events
Create events for expos, meetings, conferences, campaigns, or any networking activity and group contacts under that event.

### 4. Organizations
Create organizations and manage multiple users, teams, and events within a broader collaborative structure.

### 5. Team Collaboration
Invite members by email and allow authorized users to view shared contacts within events or organizations.

### 6. Notes and Documents
Add notes, PDFs, and other attachments to enrich each contact and preserve important context.

### 7. Structured Follow-Up
Keep contact records meaningful by attaching context, categorization, and team visibility.
